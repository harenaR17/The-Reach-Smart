## For SaaS Companies

### AI Chat Support Agent

**Hook:** Half your tickets are questions you've already answered. Stop answering them twice.

A chat agent trained on your documentation and business data that answers users and helps them troubleshoot directly — it doesn't just link to your help center, it actually solves the issue. When it hits something it can't resolve, it escalates to your team automatically.

Here's the part that compounds: once your team resolves the issue, the solution goes straight into the agent's knowledge base. Next time the same problem shows up, it handles it alone. Every escalated ticket is the last time you'll ever see it.

*The result:* a support team that only touches genuinely new problems — and there are fewer of them every month.

### Lead Finder System

**Hook:** Your salespeople should be selling. Not spending three hours a day on LinkedIn.

The system finds companies that match your ideal customer profile and pinpoints the actual decision-makers inside them — the people who sign, not just anyone with a title. Your team gets a ready-to-work list: company, contact, details.

*The result:* your sales team starts the day with conversations, not research.

### Lead Finder + Outreach System

**Hook:** From empty pipeline to replies in your inbox — without writing a single email.

Everything in the Lead Finder system, plus automated, personalized outreach. The system finds the right companies and the right people, then sends tailored emails on your behalf — written for that specific company, not a copy-paste blast. You step in only when there's genuine interest on the other end.

*The result:* qualified conversations that come to you, instead of you chasing them.

## For E-commerce Brands

### AI Chat Support Agent

**Hook:** "Where's my order?" — answered. At 2 a.m. Without you.

A chat agent that answers visitor questions, tracks orders, recommends products, and handles day-to-day customer support — escalating to your team when it can't resolve something. It remembers past resolutions and gets sharper every month.

And here's the part that directly makes money: it captures visitor details and reaches out when the product they wanted is back in stock or newly available. "Out of stock" stops being a lost sale and becomes a delayed one.

*The result:* customers taken care of around the clock, and sales that would otherwise have simply walked away.

### AI UGC Content Generator

**Hook:** New product on Monday. Ready-to-post UGC on Monday.

Generates UGC-style video content — talking-to-camera demos, testimonials — straight from your product photos and listings. Ready for social or ad creative the same day.

No hunting for creators every product launch. No two-week turnaround. No creative budget deciding how many variations you can test — and testing volume is exactly what drives your cost per result down.

*The result:* a steady stream of fresh creative, at the speed you ship products.

### AI Post-Purchase Flow System

**Hook:** The sale doesn't end at "Order confirmed." That's where the next one starts.

Automatically follows up after every purchase: order updates, personalized upsell and cross-sell offers, and review requests — each one timed to the right moment in the customer journey, not blasted out on day three.

*The result:* one-time buyers who become repeat customers, and social proof that stacks up on its own — without a single manual follow-up.

**Which automation should we start with?**
The one that returns the most, fastest — and that depends on your business. Sometimes it's support, sometimes it's pipeline, sometimes it's creative. That's exactly what the first call is for: we look at your processes and show you where the biggest leak is.

**Will customers know they're dealing with AI?**
For the customer-facing automations — some will, and that's fine. What matters is this: they get an answer instantly instead of waiting hours. And for complex cases, the system always hands off to a human.

**How long does it take to build?**

**Will it work with our existing tools?**
That's the whole point. We build around your stack — your help desk, your store, your CRM, whatever you run — not the other way around. We'll map it out together on the call.

**How much does it cost?**
Depends on what we're building — which is exactly why the first call is free. You'll leave with a clear price and a clear scope before you've spent a dollar. One thing's certain: the processes you're running manually right now are already costing you more.

**Do we need technical people on our side?**
No. We build, configure, and maintain everything. You get the results and a report of what's been done.

**What happens when the system gets something wrong?**
For the first few weeks we monitor everything and fine-tune. After that, you get regular summaries — and you always keep human control over the cases that matter, whether that's a customer conversation, an outbound email, or a published creative.
