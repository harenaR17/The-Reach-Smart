## Publishing notes

1. **Page order:** lead with Case Study 7 (your own) — it's publishable from day one and builds credibility for the rest.
2. **Anonymization that keeps credibility:** always give industry + size + market ("HR SaaS, 12-person team, US market"). "One of our clients" with no profile sounds made up even when it's true.
3. **One hero number per case:** pick the strongest metric and put it in the title. The rest are supporting evidence.
4. **The quote is mandatory** — even anonymous ("Head of Support, HR SaaS"). Pull it with the three questions from the testimonials section.
5. **Never publish a case study without real data behind it.** An empty slot on the page beats a case you can't defend when a prospect asks for details on the call.
